Delete your MegaCheck data

Last updated: July 19, 2026

You can request deletion of personal data associated with your MegaCheck app account or installation at any time.

MegaCheck currently uses anonymous or pseudonymous identifiers. Therefore, we may need information from the app to identify the correct data record.

How to request deletion

Send an email to tobias.seifert@live.de with the subject: MegaCheck Data Deletion Request.

Please include:

  • The platform you use: iOS or Android
  • Your anonymous user ID or app user ID, where available
  • A short statement confirming that you want your data deleted
  • Any additional information needed to identify the relevant account or app installation

Please do not send passwords, payment-card information or other unnecessary sensitive information.

Data that may be deleted

Depending on your use of MegaCheck, deletion may include:

  • Anonymous or pseudonymous account identifiers
  • Saved dietary preferences and allergen settings
  • App profile information stored by MegaCheck
  • Product scan history linked to your identifier, where stored
  • Unknown-product reports linked to your identifier
  • Other app-related data stored in the MegaCheck backend

Data that may be retained

Some information may be retained where required for legal, security, fraud-prevention, accounting or dispute-resolution purposes.

Anonymous product statistics that can no longer be connected to you may also be retained to improve product coverage and app reliability.

Subscriptions and purchases

Deleting MegaCheck app data does not automatically cancel an active subscription.

Subscriptions must be cancelled through the Apple App Store or Google Play account through which they were purchased.

Purchase and transaction records held by Apple, Google or other payment providers are managed under those providers’ own retention policies and legal obligations.

Processing time

We will review your request and may contact you if additional information is required to identify the relevant data.

Requests will be processed within the period required by applicable data-protection law.
